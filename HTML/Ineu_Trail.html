Ineu Peak Trail

Distance

30 km

Elevation

1.500 m

Total time

11:00:00

Description:

Embark on a breathtaking hike that starts in a beautiful forest next to a murmuring river, leading you through a verdant landscape teeming with wildflowers. As you ascend, you'll discover a serene mountain lake, a mirror to the sky and surrounding peaks. The journey intensifies with a rocky climb, testing your resolve as you approach Ineu Peak, the region's highest point.

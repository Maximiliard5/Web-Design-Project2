Rarau Peak Trail

Distance

28.5 km

Elevation

1.200 m

Total time

9:40:00

Description:

The Rarau trail offers a captivating hike starting from a tranquil village at the mountain's foot, ascending through lush meadows to stark, majestic peaks. At the summit, breathtaking views reward hikers, giving a sense of the world at your feet. The journey continues down into a dense, untouched forest, where the only sounds are nature's symphony, highlighting the beauty of the wild.
